Johnnette JF-2



The Johnnette JF-2 is ideal for low-altitude intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance missions that depend on rapid deployment and pinpoint manoeuvrability. Lightweight and simple to operate, the Johnnette JF-2 is rucksack portable and can be hand-launched for day or night observation. The Johnnette JF-2 has a gimbaled payload and delivers real-time color and/or thermal imagery to the ground control and remote viewing stations. Due to its advanced avionics, Johnnette JF-2 can be operated manually or programmed for autonomous navigation.


Design

Johnnette JF-2 with a Wingspan of 2 m and a weight of 2.6 kg, the Johnnette JF-2 provides aerial observation, day or night, at a range of up to 10 kms.


Operation

Designed for ease of operation, The Johnnette JF-2 features autonomous navigation, Autoland and an interoperable system interface.
