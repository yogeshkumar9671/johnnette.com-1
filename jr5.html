Johnnette JR-5



Johnnette JR-5 is a medium range Drone designed solely for Drone Pilot Training. JR-5 has an endurance of 30 minutes and is highly stable which makes it perfect for new pilots to train on. The Johnnette JR-5 medium range training drone that allows any new pilot to safely train and enhance his flying skills. Johnnette JR-5 model has been exempted/approved for conducting RPAS training by MoCA.
